STARDUST HARBOR

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026

In short: Stardust Harbor does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. Everything — your profile, password, and game progress — is stored only on your own device. There are no servers, no accounts in the cloud, no analytics, and no advertising trackers.

1. Who we are

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile game Stardust Harbor — Merge & Mystery ("the Game", "we", "us"), published by Vainsteel Studios. For any questions, contact us at vainsteelstudios@gmail.com.

2. What data we collect

We collect no personal data. The Game does not ask for your real name, email, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers.

The only information the Game creates is stored locally on your device and never leaves it:

  • A username and a password hash you choose to create an in-game profile (optional — you can also play as a guest).
  • Your game progress: level, coins, items, customization, story progress, daily streaks, and battle-pass status.

The password is stored only as a one-way salted hash and is used solely as a local "save slot" lock. It is not real account security — please do not reuse a password from any important account.

3. How the data is stored

All of the above is kept in your device's local storage (localStorage). It is not uploaded to any server, shared with us, or shared with third parties. If you delete the app, this data is removed by your operating system.

4. Data sharing & third parties

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties. The Game contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party trackers. It does not make network requests to send your information anywhere.

The Game loads a web font from Google Fonts for its visual style. If you'd prefer no external requests at all, the fonts can be bundled — but in the current build this is the only outbound request, and it transmits no game or personal data.

5. In-app purchases

The in-game "Orbit Pass Premium" and other upgrades are unlocked using virtual coins earned by playing. This build contains no real-money purchases. If real-money purchases are added in a future version, this policy will be updated and the store listing will declare them.

6. Children's privacy

The Game is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children. Because no data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal information of a child (or any user) for us to access.

7. Your rights

Since we hold no data about you on any server, there is nothing for us to access, export, or delete. You are in full control: clearing the app's data or uninstalling the Game removes all locally stored information.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change and the new version will be posted at this same URL.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email vainsteelstudios@gmail.com.

Hiçbir kişisel veri toplamıyoruz. Oyun; gerçek adınızı, e-postanızı, telefon numaranızı, konumunuzu, kişilerinizi, fotoğraflarınızı veya cihaz kimliklerinizi istemez.
